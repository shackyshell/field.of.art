Wrocław and Neighbourhood Discovery Tour
Enjoy a scenic drive through Lower Silesia's picturesque countryside and explore charming villages by traditional transport. Cruise along the Oder River, savor authentic Polish cuisine, and discover the region's rich history and culture.
Your pickup time will be between 8:00 and 9:00 AM, and your tour guide will reach out 15 minutes before to confirm the exact time. Enjoy an unforgettable drive through Lower Silesia along scenic country roads passing through the western region of Poland. Drive past rapeseed fields, hop plantations, fruit orchards, and traditional Polish villages.
You'll arrive at the Oder River at approximately 10:30 AM where you will board a traditional river boat past Cathedral Island, Sand Island, and Rędzin Island. Stop at the historic Ostrów Tumski to visit the stunning Gothic Cathedral and taste local honey mead, craft beer, and regional fruit liqueurs. Then take a walk through the medieval quarter and explore the charming canals to experience daily local life in Wrocław's historic districts.
Continue your boat trip along the Oder to visit a traditional gingerbread workshop in one of the neighbouring villages. Then transfer to a smaller vessel and travel along the peaceful waterways, stopping at a local Polish restaurant for traditional regional cuisine. Later on, enjoy various types of seasonal Polish pastries at a local café and listen to traditional folk music. At the end of the tour, the river boat will take you back to Wrocław's Old Town. From there the bus will drive you back to your hotel, arriving at around 4:00 PM - 4:30 PM.

Discover the enchanting Christmas atmosphere of Wrocław's historic center on foot. Experience traditional Polish holiday crafts, explore festive markets, and savor seasonal treats while learning about Silesian Christmas traditions.
Your pickup time will be between 10:00 and 11:00 AM, and your tour guide will reach out 15 minutes before to confirm the exact time. Begin your unforgettable journey at the Main Market Square (Rynek), one of Europe's largest medieval squares, beautifully decorated for the Christmas season.
Tour Path:
Start at the Main Market Square where you'll explore the famous Christmas Market, admiring handcrafted ornaments and local products. Walk past the Gothic Town Hall and see the traditional nativity scene. Continue to Ostrów Tumski (Cathedral Island) via the picturesque Oder River bridges, stopping to admire the Lock of Love Bridge and Cathedral of St. John the Baptist illuminated with holiday lights.
Cross through University Quarter along ulica Kuźnicza, passing colorful baroque townhouses and charming cafés. Head to Salt Square (Plac Solny) with its famous flower market, then walk through the Jewish Quarter discovering hidden courtyards and street art, including Wrocław's famous dwarf statues in festive attire.
At approximately 12:30 PM, arrive at a cozy traditional café where you'll participate in authentic Polish Christmas craft workshops:
Workshop Activities:

Create traditional Polish Christmas decorations and discover the unique atmosphere of holidays in Poland, Silesia, and Wrocław
Make a classic paper chain together (traditional Polish "łańcuch" decoration)
Craft ornaments from salt dough or modeling clay
Decorate your ornaments using UV nail polish or paints with varnish
Learn about Silesian Christmas traditions and family customs

After the workshop, enjoy a traditional Polish lunch featuring seasonal dishes. Later, sample various types of Polish Christmas pastries including pierniki (gingerbread), makowiec (poppy seed cake), and sernik (cheesecake) while listening to Polish Christmas carols.
Continue your walk through Nadodrze district to see the restored tenement houses, then return via Słodowa Island and the scenic Oder River promenade. End your tour back at the Main Market Square at around 4:00 PM - 4:30 PM.
